Our Story

Mark's Minerals began with a simple fascination for the Earth's natural treasures. What started as a personal collection has grown into Vermont's premier destination for mineral enthusiasts and collectors.

What Sets Us Apart

Our commitment to quality and authenticity sets us apart. Each specimen in our collection is carefully selected and verified for its geological significance and aesthetic beauty. We take pride in providing detailed information about the origin, formation, and unique characteristics of every piece.
